SO YOU

Discover what energizes you—and find your place in Ghent's student community.

So You is a prototype app designed to help students from UGent, Artevelde, and HOGENT explore their passions and connect with student associations that match their interests. Whether you're curious about tech, sports, culture, activism, or something entirely different, So You helps you discover what excites you and where you belong.
